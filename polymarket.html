Questions

Before you join

What is Polymarket?
Polymarket is a platform where people trade on the outcome of real-world events using YES/NO style contracts. Prices reflect the crowd's estimated probability. In our Polymarket track we teach the mechanics, the chart reading, and the risk management behind it. Nothing here is financial advice — all trading carries real risk of loss.
Do I need experience to join?
No. This track is built for beginners. We start from the fundamentals before moving into live charts and practice trades — no prior experience required.
Is this financial advice?
No. Everything we teach is educational. Always do your own research, and never trade with money you cannot afford to lose.
What do I need to get started?
A phone or laptop, a stable internet connection, and curiosity. A Polymarket account is helpful but completely optional until you are ready to practice with real funds.

Fill out the form below, or message us directly on WhatsApp or Telegram. We will send you everything you need for the next class.
